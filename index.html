About

Tattoos are not decoration — they're decisions.

Trinidaddy is a tattoo artist based in Spokane Washington, specializing in a diverse range of styles. With over 4 years behind the needle, she approaches every piece with the same commitment: to create something that fits you, flatters your body, and lasts a lifetime.

Whether you come in with a fully sketched concept or just a feeling you want to capture, the experience is collaborative from first contact to final wrap.
